About Happy Skin Planner

Welcome to Happy Skin Planner! Happy Skin Planner was created in order to assist people who wanted to take charge of maintaining and prioritizing their skin. It was created with love and is intended to assist you in planning out your weekly skin care routine.

With Happy Skin Planner you can decide what products you want to use by adding them into your weekly planner, checking them off when complete, and even determine what time of the day they are intended for use (e.g. Morning or Evening Routine). If you no longer wish to use a specific product, you may also remove the product from your planner. If a certain product is intended for use for a specific amount of days, you have the ability to do so. Happy Skin Planner is entirely customizable for your use in order to provide your skin with the love it needs to leave it ever so happy!

If you have a list of products you absolutely LOVE, there is a section provided for you to save those products. You may add products you would like to refer back to as well as remove products you grew apart from (I'm sorry, it's you, not me).
